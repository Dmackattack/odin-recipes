Vegan Circuit Chili

Let's see if ChatGPT can handle the difficulties of coming up with vegan alternatives to American classics! Here is what chat claims to be the world's greatest vegan chili. Does it out-taste the rest?

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, minced
  • 2 cups cooked black beans
  • 2 cups cooked kidney beans
  • 1 can (28oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 cup corn kernels
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: avocado, cilantro, lime wedges

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onions and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another minute.
  2. Add the diced bell peppers and jalapeno pepper to the pot and cook for 5 minutes until they start to soften.
  3. Add the cooked black beans, cooked kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, corn, cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper to the pot. Stir everything together.
  4. Bring the chili to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 30 minutes.
  5. Taste the chili and adjust the seasoning as needed. If the chili is too thick, you can add some water to thin it out.
  6. Serve hot with optional toppings such as sliced avocado, chopped cilantro, and lime wedges.

Enjoy your delicious and hearty vegan chili!
