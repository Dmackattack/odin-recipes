Best AI Veggie Burger

Time to get DIY and make your own veggie burgers! ChatGPT claims that this is the tastiest veggie burger that has ever come accross it's server. This protein filled burger is sure to satiate even the most ravenous of meat-eaters!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 can (15 0z) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • Optional toppings: avocado, lettuce, tomato, onion, cheese (vegan or dairy), mustard, ketchup

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked quinoa and black beans. Mash the black beans with a fork or potato masher until they are mostly broken down.
  3. In a small skillet, sauté the onion and garlic in olive oil over medium heat until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add the sautéed onion and garlic to the bowl with the quinoa and black beans. Add breadcrumbs, cilantro, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper. Mix well to combine.
  5. Form the mixture into 4 patties, about 1/2 inch thick. Place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake the patties in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, flipping them halfway through, until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  7. Toast the burger buns if desired. Place a veggie patty on each bun and add your desired toppings.

Enjoy your delicious and healthy veggie burgers!
