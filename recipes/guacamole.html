Robo Guacamole

Time to try this artificially generated guacamole recipe! You can rest knowing that CHATgpt knows how to handle itself around the kitchen, being the all knowing search engine that it is.

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe avocados
  • 1/2 red onion, sliced. Not chopped, sliced.
  • 2 small jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash the avocados with a fork or a potato masher, leaving some chunks for texture.
  3. Add the diced red onion, chopped jalapeno peppers, cilantro leaves, minced garlic, and lime juice to the bowl.
  4. Stir everything together until well combined.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Note: For extra flavor, you can also add diced tomatoes, chopped scallions, or a pinch of cumin to the guacamole. Enjoy!
